Mergers & Acquisitions

The quality of your adviser determines what is possible.

We act for founders, shareholders, boards, and corporate clients across the full range of transaction situations - business sales and founder exits, strategic acquisitions, management buyouts, cross-border combinations, and corporate carve-outs. Senior partners lead every process personally: designing the strategy, managing counterparties, and applying the judgement that marks the difference between a good outcome and the right one.

We do not manage volume. We manage your transaction.

  • Merger advisory
  • Strategic alternatives review

02

Capital Solutions

The way you capitalise a transaction is as consequential as the transaction itself.

Every transaction has a financing dimension. Whether structuring capital for an acquisition, raising growth equity at an inflection point, or refinancing ahead of a sale, we advise shareholders, boards, and management teams on how to structure, raise, and optimise capital across the full transaction lifecycle - independently of any lender relationship or balance sheet interest. Capital advisory and transaction strategy are always designed together, never as separate workstreams.

Access to capital is rarely the constraint. The quality of advice on how to structure it is.

Scope

  • Acquisition financing advisory
  • Equity capital raising
  • Debt advisory & placement
  • Structured & hybrid instruments
  • Capital structure review
  • Refinancing advisory
  • Recapitalisation advisory
  • Pre-transaction balance sheet preparation

03

Merchant Banking

Conviction expressed through capital, not just counsel.

Where we have advised on a transaction and share the long-term view of the business, we selectively invest our own capital alongside clients - as partners in the outcome, not just the process. Co-investment is available on transactions where our assessment of the business, the terms, and the management team gives us the same conviction we ask our clients to have. The structures reflect the full range of transactions we advise on: from minority equity participation to structured arrangements and long-term equity partnership.

When we invest alongside you, our interests and yours are identical. That is the point.

Scope

  • Principal co-investment
  • Minority equity participation
  • Structured equity arrangements
  • Long-term equity partnership
  • Management equity design
  • Equity rollover advisory
  • Founder & management alignment
  • Co-investor introduction
