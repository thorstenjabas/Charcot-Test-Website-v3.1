1. Privacy Policy

Who we are

Charcot Capital (UK) LLP ("Charcot Capital", "we", "us", "our") is the data controller responsible for the personal data collected through this website. Our registered office is at 18 Nevern Square, London SW5 9PD, United Kingdom.

If you have any questions about how we handle your personal data, please contact us at info@charcotcapital.com.

What data we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your telephone number (if provided)
  • Your organisation or company name (if provided)
  • The nature of your enquiry and any message you provide

We also collect standard server log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) as part of normal website operation.

Why we collect your data

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your enquiry — to contact you in connection with the matter you have raised
  • Business development — to follow up on potential advisory or investment mandates where you have expressed interest
  • Compliance — to maintain records required by applicable law

Legal basis for processing

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR). We have a legitimate interest in responding to enquiries from prospective clients and in operating our business. We do not process your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

How long we keep your data

We retain personal data from contact form submissions for up to three years from the date of receipt, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law or the enquiry leads to an ongoing mandate relationship.

Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Formspree Inc. — our contact form processor (data processed in accordance with their privacy policy and our Data Processing Agreement)
  • Professional advisers — legal, compliance, or other professional advisers where required
  • Regulatory authorities — if required by law

International transfers

Our contact form is processed by Formspree Inc., which may process data outside the European Economic Area. Formspree maintains appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Your rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability (where processing is automated and consent-based)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@charcotcapital.com. We will respond within 30 days.
