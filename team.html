Founding Partner

Jean-Benoît Terrasse

Jean-Benoît Terrasse began his investment banking career in the early 1990s and co-founded Charcot Capital in 2008, where he heads the London office. He brings more than three decades of experience advising corporates, entrepreneurs and investors on mergers and acquisitions, disposals, joint ventures, privatisations, LBOs and takeover transactions across Western Europe, the United States and a broad range of emerging markets.

Prior to Charcot Capital, Jean-Benoît held senior roles at JPMorgan and Baring Brothers. He also served as an officer in the French Navy - an experience that informs the discipline and composure he brings to complex, high-stakes mandates.

Selected transactions

Takeover bid on Worms & Cie by Artemis

Privatisation of Maroc Telecom

Disposal of Thales' testing instruments division

Acquisition of Brasseries Internationales Holdings

Disposal of General Electric's train inspection activities to Caterpillar

Founding Partner

Clément Lavallard

Clément Lavallard co-founded Charcot Capital in 2008, having spent the preceding two decades at the forefront of international investment banking. He has advised on the full spectrum of corporate finance transactions - disposals, acquisitions, mergers, joint ventures, LBOs, takeover bids and takeover defences, restructurings and complex special situations - with a particular depth of experience in cross-border and multi-jurisdictional mandates.

Clément established Charcot Capital's Singapore presence in 2011, reflecting a long-standing expertise in Asian markets and a network built through completed transactions across the region.

Selected transactions

Takeover defence and sale of Mannesmann to Vodafone

Privatisation of Maroc Telecom

Public takeover of Filofax

Disposal of Jawamanis Rafinasi to Wilmar International

Acquisition of the Salof Group by GE Oil and Gas

Partner

Marc van den Berg

Marc van den Berg joined Charcot Capital at its founding in 2008 and is based in the London office. He advises on mergers and acquisitions, disposals, joint ventures, LBOs and private investment transactions, with experience spanning both developed and emerging markets across Europe, Asia and Latin America.

Prior to joining Charcot Capital, Marc worked at Lehman Brothers, where he began his investment banking career. He brings a rigorous analytical approach to transaction structuring and a particular interest in principal investment alongside corporate and entrepreneurial clients.

Selected transactions

General Electric - disposal of assets in the transportation sector

Private investment into Nao do Brasil

Acquisition, LBO and subsequent listing of Dockwise

Director

Thorsten Jabas

Thorsten Jabas joined Charcot Capital in 2021, advising corporates, entrepreneurs and shareholders on M&A and capital raising. He plays a central role in the execution of transactions, supporting clients through the full process from preparation and positioning to negotiation and closing.

Prior to joining Charcot Capital, he held roles at Lehman Brothers, Nomura and Banco Santander, serving institutional investors on public and private transactions. He also served as CFO to high-growth software, technology and MedTech businesses. This combination of advisory and operating experience enables him to anticipate buyer requirements, shape equity stories and run disciplined, outcome-focused processes alongside senior deal leads.
