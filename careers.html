Exceptional people. Shared values.

Charcot Capital is a partner-led advisory and investment firm, deliberately small and senior-run. Mandates are led by partners, not delegated to teams - which means the work is substantive from day one. We are always interested in hearing from outstanding individuals at any stage of their career, whether experienced professionals in M&A, corporate finance or principal investing, or exceptional graduates seeking a rigorous and entrepreneurial environment. What matters most is that you share our values.

Integrity

We are trusted with sensitive mandates and confidential information. The standard for how we conduct ourselves - with clients, counterparties and each other - is absolute.

Curiosity

The firm's name was chosen deliberately. We ask pourquoi pas? - we challenge assumptions and look for routes others have overlooked. This disposition runs through everything we do.

Independent thinking

We do not follow institutional consensus. We form views, hold them with conviction, and change them when the evidence demands it - not when the room does.

Get in touch

All enquiries are treated in strict confidence and reviewed personally by a partner.
