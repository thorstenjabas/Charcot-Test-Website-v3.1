What we look for

Talent is common. Character is not.

We are always interested in hearing from outstanding individuals at any stage of their career, whether experienced professionals in M&A, corporate finance or principal investing, or exceptional graduates seeking a rigorous and entrepreneurial environment. What matters most is that you share our values.

Integrity

We are trusted with sensitive mandates and confidential information. The standard for how we conduct ourselves - with clients, counterparties and each other - is absolute.

Curiosity

The firm's name was chosen deliberately. We ask pourquoi pas? - we challenge assumptions and look for routes others have overlooked. This disposition runs through everything we do.

Independent thinking

We do not follow institutional consensus. We form views, hold them with conviction, and change them when the evidence demands it - not when the room does.

Get in touch

We do not advertise positions. We recruit through direct approaches.

If you believe you are the kind of person we are looking for, we invite you to write to us directly. Tell us about your background, what you are looking for, and why Charcot Capital in particular.

What to include

01

A brief covering note

Tell us who you are, what you are looking for, and why Charcot Capital.

02

Your CV or resume

Education, experience, and any transactions or deals you have worked on.

03

Languages and geography

We operate across multiple markets. Language skills and international experience are relevant.

All enquiries are treated in strict confidence and reviewed personally by a partner.
